Hochwall is a town located along the road to the north. It is a relatively quiet settlement where the company stopped to resupply and trade goods before continuing their trek into the colder regions of the realm.

## Notable Events

* **Strategic Resupply**: The company filled their carts with local produce here, hoping to profit from the higher prices in the hungry northern towns.
