Turmfeste is a formidable stone fortress nestled within the rocky hills of the north. It serves as a military bastion and a vital waypoint for travelers and mercenaries alike. The fortress is known for its strong discipline and its market, which often offers favorable trading prices for those willing to brave the mountain roads.

## Notable Events

* **Strategic Respite**: The company used the fortress as a waypoint to heal and trade during their trek to the eastern forests.
* **The Raven's Song**: It was during a march through these hills that the company officially raised their standard and became a true mercenary unit.
