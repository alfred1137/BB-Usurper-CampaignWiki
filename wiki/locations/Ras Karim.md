Ras Karim is a prominent southern City-State rising from the shifting dunes of the desert. It is a hub of trade and entertainment, most famous for its high-walled [Arena]({{ site.baseurl }}/Arena) where blood is spilled for the amusement of the Gilded and the profit of mercenaries. The city's markets are known for their exceptional prices, making it a favorite destination for companies looking to offload their loot.

## Notable Events

* **[Arena]({{ site.baseurl }}/Arena) Dominance**: The site of [NeCola]({{ site.baseurl }}/NeCola) and [HiYun]({{ site.baseurl }}/HiYun)'s meteoric rise to fame, where they conquered multiple bouts in a single day and earned a small fortune.
* **The Indestructible Legend**: It was here that [Bardol]({{ site.baseurl }}/Bardol) F earned his reputation by repeatedly surviving being "struck down" in the pits.
