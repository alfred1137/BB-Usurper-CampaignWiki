Gipfelfeste is a high mountain fortress city known for its jagged towers and cold, crisp air. It serves as a major hub for military and mercenary activities in the northern peaks. It was here that [The Usurper's Bane]({{ site.baseurl }}/The%20Usurper%27s%20Bane) reached its full strength of twelve members and first began to project its presence as a regional power.

## Notable Events

* **The Full Company**: The company assembled its first complete twelve-man line here, celebrating with a marching song that echoed through the cobblestone streets.
* **The Raven's Blessing**: [HiYun]({{ site.baseurl }}/HiYun) bestowed the Raven's Curse upon [Godfrey]({{ site.baseurl }}/Godfrey), marking the beginning of a new era of Abyssal power within the ranks.
* **Academic Discovery**: The company secured a rare Light Armor Textbook, beginning [Harun Al-Baji]({{ site.baseurl }}/Harun%20Al-Baji)'s education in the nimble arts.
