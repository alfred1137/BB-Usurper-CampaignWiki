Hammererden is a town of industry, defined by the constant smell of coal smoke and the rhythmic clanging of hammers against hot iron. It is a place of smiths and sturdy men, serving as a vital waypoint between the coastal towns and the mountain passes. The town's back alleys are known to hide darker secrets, including practitioners of the forbidden arts.

## Notable Events

* **The Anatomist's Den**: A site of a gruesome investigation where the company traded Bardol's physical integrity for a pulsing Unhold's Heart.
* **The Grim Tombs Discovery**: A scouting mission that led the company to the ancient and stagnant site of the Grim Tombs in the Skauna region.
