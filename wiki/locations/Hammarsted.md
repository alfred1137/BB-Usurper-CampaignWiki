Hammarsted is a large northern city that serves as a vital shelter for traders, travelers, and adventurers from the biting snow and storms of the high wastes. It is a hub of civilization in the frozen north, known for its busy markets and high-tier recruitment opportunities.

## Notable Events

* **The Smuggler's Luck**: The company discovered a legendary streak of luck in this city, pulling multiple pieces of Famed light armor from mysterious loot crates.
* **The Stuffed Bear Heist**: A bizarre contract involving the retrieval of an "immeasurable" stuffed bear for Tymmeke the Elder.
* **The Vampire Skirmish**: The company assisted local fighters in a desperate struggle against a Necromancer and a Necrosavant outside the city gates.
