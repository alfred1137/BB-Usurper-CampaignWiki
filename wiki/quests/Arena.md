The Gilded Pits of the southern city-states, primarily [Ras Karim]({{ site.baseurl }}/Ras%20Karim), where blood is the only currency that matters. The Arena offers high-paying bouts against various desert threats, from Nomads to savage beasts.

## Notable Events

* **The Five Victories**: The company successfully fulfilled an ambition to win 5 arena matches, earning a massive hoard of crowns.
* **The Legend of the Indestructible**: [Bardol]({{ site.baseurl }}/Bardol) F became a crowd favorite after surviving being "struck down" in three consecutive matches in a single day.
* **[NeCola]({{ site.baseurl }}/NeCola)'s Dominance**: The Witcher demonstrated her superior lethality by securing over fifteen kills in the pits within a 24-hour period.
