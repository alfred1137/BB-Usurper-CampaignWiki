The primary missions related to the **Rise of the Usurper (RotU)** and **Path of the [Vatt'ghern]({{ site.baseurl }}/Vatt%27ghern) (PoV)** mods.

## The Grand Mandate

The ultimate objective of [The Usurper's Bane]({{ site.baseurl }}/The%20Usurper%27s%20Bane) is the destruction of the **Usurper King**, a rogue demon lord who has betrayed the natural order and is mutating the world into a chaotic charnel house.

## Notable Special Tasks

* **The Siege of [Thal]({{ site.baseurl }}/Thal)**: Defending a major town from a directed assault by the Usurper's possessed minions.
* **The Remote Graveyard Purge**: Banishing the aetherial horrors that have claimed ancient resting places.
* **The Hunt for the Primal**: Eliminating the legendary champions who act as the Usurper's generals in the north.
