Contracts from the high-born and the powerful often carry greater risk but offer significant rewards.

## Notable Contracts

### The Staff of Foresight
The company was hired by a druidic monk and Hallstein of [Eisenstein]({{ site.baseurl }}/Eisenstein) to retrieve a holy relic from the ghost-infested Ruins of Blackwatch Keep. The mission was successful but resulted in the death of [Kaetherlen]({{ site.baseurl }}/Kaetherlen).

### The Butcher's "Favor"
A suspicious man known as "The Butcher" offered a massive sum for a "shopping trip" in [Ohnemenschen]({{ site.baseurl }}/Ohnemenschen). Sensing a trap, [HiYun]({{ site.baseurl }}/HiYun) wisely declined the offer.
