## Description

Maike is a Vala, a traveling seer, shaman, and sorceress found in the primeval forests of the north. She joined the company after sensing a gathering cataclysm that could not be faced alone. Possessing the ability to perceive and interact with the spirit world, she commands sorcerous incantations and can inscribe objects with powerful magical rune sigils. Her presence brings a mystical weight to the company, providing both spiritual guidance and arcane protection against the shadows.

## Notable Record

* **Spiritual Guide**: Joined the company specifically to prevent a foretold cataclysm.
* **Runesmith**: Capable of enchanting the company's gear with protective and offensive sigils.
* **Seer's Trance**: Can enter altered states to commune with spirits and ward off ethereal threats.
