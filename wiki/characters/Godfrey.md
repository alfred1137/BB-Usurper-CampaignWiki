## Description

Godfrey is a brawler who loves a good fight as much as he loves a strong drink. Previously employed by a local lord to fistfight royal champions, he joined the company in [Gipfelfeste]({{ site.baseurl }}/Gipfelfeste) seeking greater challenges and more lucrative work.
